About Us...

Originated in North India, The Halwai Community was engaged in the sale of legendary and authentic North Indian foodstuff and delicacies.

Including sundry eats like chaats & sweets using traditional cooking methods and secrets to retain the authentic taste and flavors.

We call this journey a visit back to the roots.
Our menu is tailored to compliment the bold as well as subtle flavors of Halwai Style Cooking.
